Features

Flashcard

Never forget a word again. Flashcards made using Kanau simplifies your study by optimizing your next review date. Hard to grasp words are repeated in the perfect interval for maximum memory retention.

Dictionary

Full access to the Japanese dictionary, complete with English translations and example sentences. Like the word? Found a sentence peculiar? Add them to your favorite deck with a click of a button.
